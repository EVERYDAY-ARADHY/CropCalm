CROP-CALM PROJECT: HARDWARE SHOPPING LIST
=========================================

Since you already have the NodeMCU, sensors (HC-SR04/PIR), LED, Buzzer, Piezo, 4500mAh battery, TP4056, XL6009E1, and Raspberry Pi, you only need to buy these cheap electronic components to finish the circuit:

1. NPN TRANSISTORS
   - Model: BC547 or 2N2222
   - Quantity: 5 to 10
   - Purpose: Acts as an amplifier driver to make the 40kHz Piezo disc actually loud.

2. RESISTORS (220 OHM)
   - Value: 220Ω
   - Quantity: 5
   - Purpose: Protects your LEDs from burning out over time.

3. RESISTORS (1k OHM)
   - Value: 1kΩ
   - Quantity: 5
   - Purpose: Protects the NPN transistor base from pulling too much current from the ESP8266.

4. FLYBACK DIODES
   - Model: 1N4148
   - Quantity: 5
   - Purpose: Protects your ESP8266 board from voltage spikes caused by the Piezo vibrating.

5. (OPTIONAL) MICROWAVE RADAR
   - Model: RCWL-0516
   - Quantity: 1
   - Purpose: Active motion detection (upgrades the PIR sensor because it has no blind spots).

6. MASTER TOGGLE SWITCH
   - Model: Basic SPST Mini Toggle Switch or Rocker Switch
   - Quantity: 1
   - Purpose: Main power ON/OFF switch for the edge node to save battery during transport.
